# Task 2 – AI UGC Ad Script Generator

## About the Task
Modern brands get better results with UGC (User Generated Content) style ads because they look natural and authentic. These ads feel like real customer experiences instead of traditional advertisements. In this task, a prompt system is designed to generate UGC-style ad scripts using AI.

---

# Product Chosen

Product Name: GlowSkin Vitamin C Face Serum  
Business Type: D2C Skincare Brand  
Target Audience: Young adults interested in skincare and glowing skin.

---

# Tool Used

AI Tool: ChatGPT

---

# Structured Prompt Framework

Act as a UGC content creator for Instagram ads.

Create a short UGC-style ad script for a skincare product called "GlowSkin Vitamin C Face Serum".

The ad should follow this structure:
1. Hook – Grab attention in the first 3 seconds
2. Problem – Common skin problem people face
3. Solution – Introduce the product
4. Benefits – Key advantages of the product
5. Call-To-Action – Encourage viewers to try the product

Make the script sound natural, like a real customer sharing their experience.
