# Task 3 – AI SEO Blog & Content Cluster Generator

## About the Task
Many business websites fail to attract visitors because their content is not optimized for search engines. SEO content requires a structured strategy, including a pillar blog and multiple supporting blogs that target related keywords. This task demonstrates how AI prompt engineering can generate SEO blog structures and content clusters for business websites.

# Structured Prompt Framework

Act as an SEO content strategist.

Create SEO-optimized blog content for a dental clinic website called "SmileCare Dental Clinic" located in Bangalore.

Include:

1. A pillar blog targeting the keyword "Best Dental Clinic in Bangalore".
2. 3–4 supporting blogs related to dental treatments and patient questions.
3. Use H1, H2, and H3 headings for SEO.
4. Include helpful information, clear sections, and internal linking suggestions.
5. Add a business-focused conclusion encouraging patients to visit the clinic.

Ensure the blog content is informative, SEO-friendly, and easy to read.
