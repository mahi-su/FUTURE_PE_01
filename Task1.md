# Task 1 – AI Website Copy Generator for Local Businesses

## About the Task
Many local businesses lose potential customers because their website content is unclear, outdated, or lacks a strong value proposition. Hiring professional copywriters can be expensive for small businesses. By using AI and prompt engineering, businesses can generate high-quality website copy quickly and effectively.

This task demonstrates how AI can be used to create structured website content for local businesses.

---

## Business Chosen

Business Name: Brew Haven Cafe  
Location: Bangalore  
Business Type: Local Cafe  

Services:
- Coffee & beverages
- Bakery items
- Breakfast menu

---

## Tool Used

AI Tool: ChatGPT

---

## Prompt Framework

Act as a professional website copywriter.

Create website copy for a local cafe called **"Brew Haven Cafe"** located in Bangalore.

Include:

1. Homepage headline and introduction  
2. Service descriptions  
3. Call-to-action encouraging customers to visit  

Make the tone friendly, engaging, and suitable for a business website.

---

## Homepage Copy

### Headline
Brew Haven Cafe – Your Perfect Coffee Spot in Bangalore

### Subheadline
Fresh coffee, delicious snacks, and a cozy place to relax.

### Introduction
Welcome to **Brew Haven Cafe**, where great coffee meets a warm and relaxing atmosphere. We serve freshly brewed coffee, homemade bakery items, and delicious breakfast options that make every visit special. Whether you're meeting friends, working remotely, or just enjoying a quiet break, Brew Haven Cafe is the perfect place to unwind.
